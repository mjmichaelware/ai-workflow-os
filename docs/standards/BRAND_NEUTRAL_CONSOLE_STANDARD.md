# Brand Neutral Console Standard

AI Workflow OS must look professional without imitating another company product or using another company product name as an interface style.

## Rules

- Do not describe the UI as another company product style.
- Do not place competitor product styling phrases in code, tests, docs, or UI copy.
- Use neutral product language such as infrastructure-grade, device-native console, local operator, control surface, and public operator.
- Integration names are allowed only when describing real integrations, tools, or capability inventory.
- Public text must stay clean, professional, contributor-safe, and brand-neutral.
