# Operating System / Generated App Boundary

AI Workflow OS is the self-building operating agent.
Generated apps are material outputs.

## The OS may

- plan apps
- generate apps
- test apps
- export apps
- inspect app manifests
- update app templates
- preserve proof and audit logs

## Generated apps must not

- overwrite AI Workflow OS internals
- shadow existing OS modules
- claim fake proof
- silently swallow button failures
- print or commit secrets

## Direction

Inside out means OS standards generate app structure.
Outside in means observed app failures update OS standards and future templates.
Backwards means every failure becomes a prevention rule.
